❓ How to Play

  • 🎯 Aim and click (or tap) to shoot bubbles.
  • 🔗 Match 3 or more bubbles of the same colour to pop them.
  • 💰 Earn coins for popped bubbles and level clears.
  • 💥 Floating bubbles fall after a match — bonus points!
  • ❤️ You have 3 lives. Lose one each time bubbles reach the danger line.
  • 🏆 Beat all 10 levels to become the Bubble Champion!
